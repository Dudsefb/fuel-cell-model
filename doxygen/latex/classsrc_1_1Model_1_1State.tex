\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\begin{DoxyVerb}A class to facilitate the storage and transfer of model thermodynamic state information.

The thermodynamic state is understood to be the:
    @param T Temperature
    @param P Pressure
    @param pH2 Hydrogen partial pressure
    @param pO2 Oxygen partial pressure
    @param pH2O Water partial pressure
\end{DoxyVerb}
